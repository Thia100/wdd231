Site plan

Aced:

I chose the name "Aced" because it reflects the goal of helping students excel academically by staying organized, managing their study schedules, and providing study tips.

Site purpose:

The site would provide study tips to help students stay organized. It would also include a study planner that allows users to add a subject, assign a due date and a complete status. As well as a a dynamic motivational quotes division.

Scenerio questions

How can I keep track of all my assignments and avoid missing deadlines?

Use the study planner to add each assignment, set its due date, and mark it as complete when finished. This helps you stay organized and prioritize your workload.

What study techniques can help me prepare more effectively for an upcoming exam?

Visit the study tips section to learn strategies such as active recall, spaced repetition, time management, and note-taking methods that can improve your understanding and retention.

I often lose motivation while studying. How can this website help me stay focused?

The website provides motivational quotes and productivity tips to encourage consistent study habits. You can also use the planner to break large tasks into smaller, manageable goals.

Can I organize my study schedule for multiple subjects in one place?

Yes. The study planner allows you to add tasks for different subjects, assign due dates, and track your progress so you can manage your time more effectively and stay on top of all your courses.
