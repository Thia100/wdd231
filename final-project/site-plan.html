FAQ'S

How can I Book appointments

Click on the button on the homepage that says "Book Now" and fill the form provided or navigate to our services page, fill the form and click on "Book Appointment" button

How can I Shop

To shop, navigate to our shop tab, select the items you want(items will automatically add to cart) and pay with your prefered method

How can I get in touch with you?

Check out our social media links at the top and bottom of the page. If you're using a mobile phone, open the hamburger menu and select "contact us"
