About Us

The Osun State Chamber of Commerce serves as the voice of businesses across the state, connecting entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders to opportunities for growth. We promote trade, investment, and innovation by creating a platform where businesses can collaborate, access resources, and build strong networks.

Through advocacy, training, and partnerships, we support both small enterprises and large organizations, ensuring they thrive in a competitive economy. Rooted in Osun's rich cultural and economic heritage, we are committed to driving sustainable development and empowering businesses to contribute to the prosperity of the state.

Latest News

The festival of Osun-Osgobo, which takes place every year in Osogbo, Nigeria, celebrates the goddess of fertility, Osun. The festival renews the contract between humans and the divine: Osun offers grace to the community; in return, it vows to honor her Sacred Grove. This ceremony is part of a rich indigenous Yoruba religious tradition that began in West Africa and has become one of the ten largest religions in the world, with upwards of 100 million practitioners.
