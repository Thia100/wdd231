Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry is dedicated to supporting businesses across Lagos and Nigeria with the resources, advocacy, and connections needed to thrive in a dynamic economy. From promoting trade and investment opportunities to empowering entrepreneurs and enterprises, the Chamber provides businesses with the support they need for sustainable growth and success.

Advocacy & Representation

We advocate for policies that support business growth, innovation, and a stronger business environment. We keep members informed on regulatory changes and represent their interests in discussions that shape commerce and industry.

Compliance

Running a business comes with many responsibilities, and staying compliant with business regulations is essential for growth and success. The Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industry provides trusted guidance, resources, and support to help businesses operate confidently and focus on what matters most, growing their business.
